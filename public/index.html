HackUTD Game Jam
Oct 25-31st
Welcome to HackUTD GameJam! Our GameJam is a week long event for students to learn how to develop games. This GameJam is beginner-friendly and we will be inviting numerous student organizations to provide mentors and workshops to guide you!
Click the icons to submit a game, join a workshop, or view the process stream.
Submit Game
Submit your game by October 30th 11:59pm.
Workshops
Several workshops are hosted by participating organizations. Workshop topics include: TBD.
Streaming
During the game jam organizations will be hosting a "process stream." As a participant you can tune into the live stream to watch an organization's game development process.
